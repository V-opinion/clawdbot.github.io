Can I use it with my existing messaging apps?

Yes! Clawdbot supports WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, iMessage, Signal, and many more platforms.

Is my data private?

When self-hosted, all data stays on your infrastructure. Conversations with Claude are subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.

Basic command-line knowledge helps, but the setup wizard makes it accessible for beginners.
