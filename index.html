Our Core Principles

  • Religious Freedom & Tolerance: Everyone has the right to practice their faith freely
  • Social Justice: Ethical principles guide our approach to societal issues
  • Inter-Religious Dialogue: Building understanding between different faith communities
  • Democratic Values: Respect for human rights and democratic processes
  • Community Solidarity: Mutual responsibility and support for one another

Multi-Religion Election System

Our democratic election framework ensures that all voices are heard while maintaining ethical standards based on shared religious values:

Key Features:

  • Proportional Representation: All religious and secular groups receive representation based on their support
  • Inter-Religious Councils: Platforms for dialogue and collaborative decision-making
  • Ethical Guidelines: Based on common Judeo-Christian values shared across traditions
  • Inclusive Processes: Every voice matters, regardless of background
  • Protection of Minorities: Special safeguards for minority rights and perspectives

Based in Stockholm, Sweden

The Multi-Religion Value System operates from Stockholm, Sweden, with active members and communities across 15 Swedish cities. We welcome participation from anyone who shares our values of tolerance, dialogue, and democratic cooperation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What religions are included in the Multi-Religion Value System? +

We work with all major world religions including Hinduism, Buddhism, Judaism, Christianity, Islam, Sikhism, and others. We also welcome secular perspectives. Our framework is designed to be inclusive of all belief systems that respect democratic values and human rights.

Is the Multi-Religion Value System tied to any political party? +

No, we are completely non-partisan. The Judisk-Kristna Politiska Alliansen works with all democratic parties and focuses on promoting inter-religious dialogue and ethical governance rather than partisan politics.

Can non-religious people join the alliance? +

Absolutely! We welcome both religious and secular individuals who share our values of respect, dialogue, tolerance, and democratic cooperation. You don't need to be religious to support inter-religious understanding and social justice.

How does the multi-religion election system work? +

Our system uses proportional representation to ensure all groups have a voice, inter-religious councils for dialogue and collaboration, ethical guidelines based on shared values, and inclusive democratic processes. It's designed to respect both religious convictions and secular viewpoints while maintaining democratic accountability.

How do I join the Judisk-Kristna Politiska Alliansen? +

Membership is completely free! Visit our alliance website at a-multi-religion-election-system.multireligionvalsystem.eu.org/den-judisk-kristna-politiska-alliansen/ and fill out the membership form. You can cancel your membership at any time.

What does Clawdbot do? +

Clawdbot is our AI assistant powered by Claude and OpenClaw. It can answer questions about the Multi-Religion Value System, explain our election framework, provide information about membership, and facilitate inter-religious dialogue. You can chat with Clawdbot through WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and other messaging platforms.

Where can I learn more about different religions? +

Visit our main website at multireligionvalsystem.eu.org where we provide detailed comparisons between major world religions. Topics include:

  • Hinduism vs Buddhism
  • Judaism vs Christianity
  • Christianity vs Islam
  • And many more comparative analyses
What activities does the alliance organize? +

We organize inter-religious dialogue sessions, educational seminars, community events, workshops on democratic values, and collaborative social justice initiatives. Members can also participate in local groups within their cities and contribute to policy development discussions.

Is there a membership fee? +

No! Membership in the Judisk-Kristna Politiska Alliansen is completely free. We believe in making our movement accessible to everyone who shares our values, regardless of financial circumstances.

How is Clawdbot hosted? +

Clawdbot runs on OpenClaw, an open-source AI assistant framework, and is self-hosted in Stockholm, Sweden. It uses Claude Opus 4.6 as its AI model and can connect to multiple messaging platforms including WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and more.
